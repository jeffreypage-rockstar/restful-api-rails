Share a discovery, an object, a moment; ask a question, tell a joke, showcase a finding, spread your knowledge, express yourself.

Create and follow #hashtag buckets

A new way to look at hashtags.

Each #hashtag is a bucket that users can post to. Easily find and follow the ones that interest you, discover trending content, or create your own and find your community.

You always get the best

Posts and comments are socially curated. The better your post does on Hyper, the further towards the front of the feed it will go, and more people will see it. The better your comment does, the further up in the list it will go.

Published online

Not only is sharing posts to Facebook, Twitter, or Tumblr just a tap away, but every post can be accessed via a unique URL, to make for super easy sharing.
